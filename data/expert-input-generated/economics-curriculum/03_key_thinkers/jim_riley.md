# Jim Riley

**Full Name:** Jim Riley

**Current Role:** Co-Founder and Director of tutor2u (1999-present)

**Known For:** Pioneering digital resources for Economics education; development of exam technique frameworks for A-Level Economics; building the UK's leading online curriculum support platform for Business and Economics teachers; innovative approaches to blended learning and digital pedagogy in secondary and post-16 education.

---

## Biography and Career History

Jim Riley co-founded tutor2u in 1999 alongside brothers Geoff Riley and Dave Riley, initially as a website providing revision resources for A-Level Business Studies and Economics students. The venture began during the early expansion of internet-based educational resources and has since grown into one of the most comprehensive digital platforms supporting UK secondary education, particularly in Economics and Business.

Riley's background combines commercial experience with educational technology expertise. Before establishing tutor2u, he worked in marketing and business development roles, which informed his understanding of how to create accessible, student-friendly educational content that could be delivered at scale through digital platforms. This commercial acumen proved instrumental in developing tutor2u's sustainable business model, which balances free resources with premium content and professional development offerings.

Under Riley's leadership as a director, tutor2u expanded significantly beyond its initial focus. The platform now serves over 1.5 million students and teachers annually across multiple subjects, though Economics and Business remain core specialisms. The company produces extensive written resources, video content, podcasts, and interactive materials aligned with major examination board specifications including AQA, Edexcel, OCR, and Eduqas.

Riley has been particularly influential in developing tutor2u's teacher support infrastructure. The platform offers Continuing Professional Development (CPD) courses, live webinars, and revision workshops that reach thousands of educators and students each year. His vision emphasised making high-quality Economics education accessible regardless of school resources, addressing educational inequality through digital means.

Throughout the 2000s and 2010s, Riley oversaw tutor2u's response to major curriculum changes, including the transition from modular to linear A-Levels in Economics (2015) and the increased emphasis on quantitative skills. The platform developed comprehensive resources addressing these reforms, producing specification-specific materials that became widely adopted across UK schools and colleges.

Riley's contribution to Economics education extends beyond content creation. He has been a prominent advocate for improving exam technique teaching, arguing that understanding economic concepts alone is insufficient without developing robust analytical and evaluative skills. This philosophy underpins much of tutor2u's pedagogical approach.

His work has been recognised through tutor2u's various awards and partnerships with examination boards, universities, and educational organisations. The platform has become an established part of the UK Economics education landscape, with materials regularly referenced in departmental schemes of work and recommended by teachers' professional networks.

## Key Ideas and Frameworks

### 1. The "Chain of Reasoning" Approach to Exam Technique

Riley has championed a structured methodology for developing extended answers in Economics examinations, particularly for essay and data-response questions. This approach emphasises the importance of developing arguments through logical sequences: stating a point, explaining the economic mechanism, applying context, and developing consequences. The framework addresses a common weakness in student responses—assertions without economic reasoning.

The Chain of Reasoning method explicitly teaches students to build answers systematically, incorporating economic terminology, diagrams where appropriate, and explicit links back to the question's context. This technique has been widely adopted across UK Economics departments and is embedded throughout tutor2u's examination preparation materials. The approach aligns with mark schemes from major examination boards, which reward sustained analytical chains and developed evaluation.

### 2. Digital-First Curriculum Resource Design

Riley pioneered approaches to curriculum resource design specifically optimised for digital consumption by students and teachers. Rather than simply digitising traditional textbook formats, tutor2u's materials employ shorter, focused content pieces with embedded multimedia, hyperlinked interconnections between topics, and regular updates to incorporate contemporary economic events.

This pedagogical philosophy recognises that digital learning requires different content structuring than print-based resources. Materials are designed for both classroom delivery via interactive whiteboards and independent student revision via mobile devices. The approach emphasises accessibility through clear visual hierarchies, consistent formatting, and multiple content formats (written, video, audio) addressing diverse learning preferences.

Riley's digital-first approach anticipated the shift toward blended learning that accelerated during the COVID-19 pandemic, meaning tutor2u's resources were particularly valuable when schools moved to remote teaching in 2020-2021.

### 3. "Current Economics" Integration

Riley has consistently advocated for integrating contemporary economic events and data into teaching practice. Tutor2u produces regular blog posts, videos, and teaching resources responding to economic developments within days or weeks of their occurrence. This approach makes Economics education more engaging and relevant whilst demonstrating how theoretical concepts apply to real-world situations.

Materials have covered topics including Brexit implications, COVID-19 economic responses, inflation dynamics, labour market changes, and environmental economics developments. This "current economics" philosophy addresses student engagement challenges by showing Economics as a living, dynamic discipline rather than abstract theory.

The approach requires significant ongoing content production but has proven influential in showing teachers how to incorporate contemporary material into established curricula. Tutor2u's economic updates are widely used as starter activities, case studies, and discussion prompts in Economics classrooms.

### 4. Systematic Topic Coverage Through "Study Notes"

Riley oversaw development of tutor2u's comprehensive "study notes" system—detailed topic summaries organised by specification for major examination boards. These resources provide structured coverage of syllabus content with clear explanations, relevant diagrams, and application examples.

The study notes framework reflects Riley's understanding that effective revision resources must balance comprehensiveness with accessibility. Each note targets specific specification requirements whilst remaining concise enough for realistic student engagement. The materials include examination tips, common misconceptions, and links to related topics, supporting both independent learning and teacher-directed study.

These study notes have become extensively used across UK schools, particularly in departments with limited resources or non-specialist Economics teachers. They provide a reliable foundation ensuring syllabus coverage whilst allowing teachers to supplement with additional materials and activities.

### 5. Knowledge Retrieval and Spaced Practice Integration

Influenced by cognitive science research on effective learning, Riley championed incorporating knowledge retrieval practice into tutor2u's resources. The platform offers extensive question banks, flashcards, and quizzes designed to support spaced repetition and active recall—evidence-based learning strategies shown to improve long-term retention.

This approach moves beyond passive revision methods toward active engagement with content. Materials are structured to enable regular low-stakes testing, helping students identify knowledge gaps and strengthen memory retrieval pathways. The integration of these evidence-informed practices demonstrates Riley's commitment to pedagogically sound resource design rather than merely producing content.

### 6. The "Economics Skills" Framework

Riley emphasised developing students' broader economic competencies beyond content knowledge. Tutor2u's resources explicitly address skills including quantitative methods, diagram construction and interpretation, evaluation techniques, and critical analysis of economic policies.

The Economics Skills framework recognises that A-Level Economics (particularly post-2015 reforms) requires significant mathematical competency. Tutor2u produces targeted materials supporting quantitative skills development, including calculating elasticities, interpreting statistical data, understanding index numbers, and analysing economic indicators. These resources address common anxiety around mathematical content amongst Economics students.

Similarly, evaluation skills receive systematic attention. Resources teach students frameworks for balancing arguments, considering stakeholder perspectives, assessing short-term versus long-term impacts, and reaching evidenced judgements—skills explicitly rewarded in examination mark schemes but often inadequately taught.

### 7. Teacher CPD and Professional Learning Communities

Riley recognised that high-quality resources alone were insufficient without supporting teacher development. Tutor2u established comprehensive CPD provision including subject-specific training, exam board updates, and pedagogical technique workshops.

The platform facilitates professional learning communities through conferences, online forums, and networking events where Economics teachers share practice and discuss curriculum challenges. These initiatives address teacher isolation, particularly in smaller departments, and support continuing professional development in a subject where many teachers lack formal Economics qualifications beyond undergraduate level.

CPD offerings specifically address challenges such as teaching unfamiliar syllabus content following curriculum changes, developing students' quantitative skills, and implementing effective assessment practices. Riley's approach views teacher development as integral to improving student outcomes.

### 8. Examination Board Alignment and Specification Mapping

Under Riley's direction, tutor2u developed systematic approaches to mapping resources against specific examination board requirements. Materials are explicitly labelled by specification, ensuring teachers can quickly identify relevant content for their particular syllabus.

This specification-driven approach reflects understanding of practical teaching realities—teachers need resources that clearly align with their examination requirements rather than generic content. Tutor2u produces parallel materials for different boards where specifications diverge, particularly important given variations in assessment approaches and optional topic coverage.

The detailed specification mapping extends to assessment objectives, helping teachers ensure appropriate balance between knowledge, application, analysis, and evaluation in their teaching and assessment practices.

## Influence on Curriculum and Policy

### Impact on A-Level Economics Teaching Practice

Riley's work through tutor2u has substantially influenced how A-Level Economics is taught across the UK. The platform's resources have achieved near-ubiquitous adoption in Economics departments, with materials regularly cited in schemes of work and departmental resource lists. Surveys of Economics teachers consistently identify tutor2u among the most-used resources, alongside official textbooks.

The standardisation of certain teaching approaches—particularly around exam technique and diagrammatic analysis—partly reflects tutor2u's influential pedagogical frameworks. Riley's emphasis on structured analytical development and explicit evaluation technique has shaped how many teachers approach these challenging aspects of Economics instruction.

### Supporting Curriculum Transitions

Tutor2u played a significant role during the major A-Level reforms implemented from 2015, when Economics transitioned from modular to linear assessment with enhanced quantitative requirements. Riley ensured early production of resources aligned with new specifications, providing crucial support for departments navigating substantial curriculum changes.

The platform's comprehensive coverage of new content areas and assessment approaches helped smooth the transition, particularly for smaller departments with limited resources. Tutor2u's webinars and guidance materials explaining specification changes reached thousands of teachers during this transition period.

### Addressing Educational Inequality Through Digital Access

Riley's vision of freely accessible digital resources has implications for educational equity. By providing high-quality materials without paywalls (alongside premium content), tutor2u enables students from schools with limited budgets to access equivalent resources to those in well-funded institutions. This democratising effect has been particularly significant in Economics, where textbook costs can present barriers.

During the COVID-19 pandemic (2020-2021), when schools moved to remote learning, tutor2u's established digital infrastructure proved invaluable. The platform rapidly expanded free resource access and produced guidance for teaching Economics online, supporting teachers navigating unprecedented challenges.

### Examination Board Relationships

Whilst maintaining independence, tutor2u has developed working relationships with major examination boards including AQA, Edexcel, OCR, and Eduqas. Riley has overseen production of board-approved resources and facilitated board representatives delivering CPD through tutor2u's platforms. ### Influence on Digital Pedagogy Discourse

Beyond Economics specifically, Riley's work demonstrates successful large-scale implementation of digital educational resources. Tutor2u's model has influenced discussions around blended learning, flipped classroom approaches, and online curriculum support across multiple subjects. The platform's sustainability has proven that high-quality educational technology ventures can succeed commercially whilst maintaining educational integrity.

## Pedagogical Approach and Philosophy

### Evidence-Informed Practice

Riley's approach to resource development reflects engagement with educational research, particularly cognitive science findings on effective learning. Integration of spaced practice, retrieval practice, and dual coding principles demonstrates commitment to evidence-informed pedagogy rather than untested approaches.

The platform's emphasis on active learning strategies—problem-solving, application tasks, and analytical questions—aligns with research showing passive revision methods produce limited retention. This theoretical grounding distinguishes tutor2u's resources from simple content repositories.

### Accessibility and Inclusive Design

Resources developed under Riley's leadership emphasise accessible design through clear language, consistent structure, and multiple representation formats. Materials avoid unnecessary complexity whilst maintaining intellectual rigour appropriate for A-Level study. This approach supports students with diverse learning needs without requiring separate differentiated versions.

The provision of content in written, video, and audio formats enables students to engage through their preferred modalities. Transcripts accompany videos, supporting students with hearing impairments or those preferring written content.

### Assessment for Learning Integration

Riley championed embedding formative assessment opportunities throughout resources. Regular checkpoints, practice questions, and self-assessment tools enable students to monitor their understanding and identify areas requiring further study. This philosophy reflects assessment for learning principles emphasising feedback and metacognitive development.

Teacher resources include assessment materials with detailed mark schemes and examiner commentary, supporting effective formative assessment practices in classrooms. The emphasis on helping students understand mark scheme requirements and assessment objectives addresses the often-opaque nature of examination assessment.

### Student-Centred Content Design

Tutor2u's resources reflect student-centred design philosophy through clear learning objectives, structured progressions, and explicit connections to examination requirements. Materials anticipate common misconceptions and difficulties, addressing these proactively through explanations and examples.

The tone of resources balances academic rigour with accessibility, avoiding overly formal language that might alienate students whilst maintaining appropriate subject terminology. This approach reflects Riley's commercial background—understanding the "customer" perspective whilst delivering educational quality.

### Continuous Improvement and Responsiveness

Riley established practices of continuous resource updating based on user feedback, examination outcomes, and curriculum developments. This iterative approach ensures materials remain current and effective rather than becoming static products. Regular content reviews and updates distinguish tutor2u from traditional textbook publishing models.

The platform's responsiveness to teacher and student feedback creates an evolving resource base informed by actual classroom use. This practitioner-focused development process ensures materials address real teaching challenges rather than theoretical ideals.
